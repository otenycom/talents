# Packing Templates (shipped reference data — seed `todos`, never per-tenant state)

Generic starter packing lists by trip type. The `trip-planner` seeds these as `todos`
(category `packing`) when a trip is created or the tenant asks "what do I need to pack?"
(`trip-planner/references/todos.md`). They are **starting points** — tailor to the
destination, season, length, and the tenant's `traveller_prefs`; never present as
exhaustive. Nothing here is tenant-specific.

## Core (every trip)
- Passport / ID, boarding passes, booking confirmations (see `document-checklists.md`)
- Phone + charger + power bank; travel plug adapter for the destination
- Payment cards + a little local cash; note your bank's travel/usage settings
- Medications (in carry-on, in original packaging) + a small first-aid kit
- Reusable water bottle; sunglasses; a day bag

## Beach / sun
- Swimwear ×2, beach towel, flip-flops, cover-up/hat
- High-SPF sunscreen, after-sun, aloe
- Light breathable clothing; one warmer layer for evenings
- Snorkel/goggles if relevant; dry bag for valuables

## City break
- Comfortable walking shoes (broken in)
- Layers + a compact rain jacket / umbrella
- One smart-casual outfit for dinners
- Day bag with anti-theft zip; offline maps downloaded

## Ski / cold
- Base layers, mid layers, waterproof outer shell + ski trousers
- Gloves, hat, neck gaiter, ski socks ×2+
- Goggles, helmet (or rental note), high-SPF + lip balm
- Hand/foot warmers; après-ski footwear

## Road trip
- Driving licence (+ international permit if required — verify), car documents
- Phone car mount + charger, offline maps
- Snacks, water, reusable bags; a small cooler
- Basic car kit: warning triangle/hi-vis (legally required in many countries — verify),
  tyre/pressure check before departure

## With kids
- Each child's documents (some countries need consent letters for one-parent travel — verify)
- Snacks, refillable bottles, entertainment (downloaded), comfort item
- Spare clothes in carry-on, wipes, any child meds
- Stroller/carrier; car seat plan (bring vs rent — confirm with the rental)

## Business
- Laptop + charger, presentation adapter (HDMI/USB-C), notebook
- Smart outfits + a backup shirt in carry-on; shoe care
- Business cards; chargers/cables organised in a pouch
- Receipts pouch/app for expenses; any per-diem notes
